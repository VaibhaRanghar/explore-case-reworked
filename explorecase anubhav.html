pilgrimage circuit

The Char Dham Yatra — four sacred shrines in the Garhwal Himalayas — is one of Hinduism's most auspicious pilgrimages, undertaken by lakhs of devotees each year.

At Explore Case, we've guided hundreds of families and pilgrims through this divine journey. Every package is planned meticulously so you can focus entirely on prayer and the breathtaking Himalayan landscape.

Yamunotri

The source of the sacred Yamuna river, Yamunotri is set at 3,293m in Uttarkashi. Pilgrims trek 6km from Janki Chatti through dense forests and hot water springs to reach the revered temple of Goddess Yamuna.

FAQ

Common questions

When is the best time for Char Dham Yatra?

The shrines open in April/May and close in October/November. May–June and September–October are ideal — post-monsoon views are spectacular.

How do I book a Kedarnath helicopter?

Contact us directly. We handle all bookings, permits, and shuttles from Phata/Sirsi helipad. Advance booking is strongly recommended.

Do you offer custom group packages?

Yes — groups of all sizes. We customize itineraries, negotiate group hotel rates, and arrange dedicated vehicles and guides.

What is your cancellation policy?

Full refund for cancellations 15+ days before departure. Partial refund within 7–14 days. We also help reschedule where possible.
